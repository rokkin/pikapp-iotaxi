About Us

Our Mission

Pi Kappa Phi was founded on the principle of creating exceptional leaders. The Iota Xi Chapter at UT Dallas upholds this tradition by fostering a brotherhood committed to academic excellence, leadership development, and service to others. We believe in building men of character who will make a positive impact on their communities and the world.

Our Values

Our chapter is built on the pillars of Common Loyalty, Personal Responsibility, and Achievement. We cultivate an environment where brothers support each other's growth, hold themselves accountable to high standards, and strive for excellence in all endeavors. Through our commitment to The Ability Experience, we demonstrate that true leadership means empowering others.

The Ability Experience is Pi Kappa Phi's own philanthropy, dedicated to empowering people with disabilities to achieve their goals through shared experiences. Our chapter is committed to raising awareness and funds to support programs that build confident leaders and promote inclusion. Every contribution helps create life-changing experiences for people with disabilities across the nation.
